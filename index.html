Arya College of Engineering, Mumbai was established in 2004 with the aim of providing quality technical education to students. The college is managed by the Arya Education Trust and is approved by AICTE, New Delhi, and the Government of Maharashtra. It is affiliated with the University of Mumbai and has an ISO 9001:2015 Certification. The college offers undergraduate courses in Computer Engineering, Information Technology, Electronics and Telecommunication, and Mechanical Engineering, as well as postgraduate courses in Computer Engineering and Information Technology. The college has received accreditation from the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) for its undergraduate programs in Computer Engineering and Information Technology and has been awarded a grade of 'A' (3.16 CGPA) by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC).
